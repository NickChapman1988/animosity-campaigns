What is Animosity Campaigns

Featuring team strategy and collaborative storytelling, Animosity Campaigns invites you to forge your narrative alongside players from around the world in the internet's best WARHAMMER Age of Sigmar global narrative events! From 2004 to 2014, Animosity Campaigns was the internet's premier Narrative Play community for WARHAMMER Fantasy Battles. Now on our fourth major global narrative event, Team Animosity has been working to bring this incredible experience into the Age of Sigmar since 2019.
